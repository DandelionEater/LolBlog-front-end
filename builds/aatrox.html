Champion's lore

Once honored defenders of Shurima against the Void, Aatrox and his brethren would eventually become an even greater threat to Runeterra, and were defeated only by cunning mortal sorcery. But after centuries of imprisonment, Aatrox was the first to find freedom once more, corrupting and transforming those foolish enough to try and wield the magical weapon that contained his essence. Now, with stolen flesh, he walks Runeterra in a brutal approximation of his previous form, seeking an apocalyptic and long overdue vengeance.

Recommended runes

As of today Aatrox builds mainly bruiser items, tanky items are optional depending on your match-up.

Let's start with runes:
Primary rune page is going to be „Precision“. Keystone „Conqueror“ then follow it by choosing „Triump“, „Legend: Alacrity and finish it off with „Last stand“.
Secondary rune page is going to be „Resolve“. Choose „Second Wind“, „Overgrowth“.
As for the masteries, pick „Offense: adaptive force“, „Flex: adaptive force“, „Defense: health“.

Recommended skills

Let's talk skills!
Start by levelling your Q level 1, then E level 2 and W level 3. After that just level your skills in that order „Q > E > W“ and don't forget to put a point on your R skill when reaching levels 6/11/16.

Recommended items

It's time to discuss the itemization!
As we have mentioned before Aatrox builds mainly bruiser items. So let's start with your starting items.
Generally you want to buy a „health potion“ and a „Doran's shield“.


Next is your core items (meaning you have to build every game).
Start off with „Eclipse“ then get „Plated steelcaps“ and „Sundered sky“.


Now we get to items that will be decided based on your.
Your fourth item can be picked in between „Sterak's gage“ or „Serylda's grudge“.
Picking „Sterak's gage“ is recommended whenever your enemy is either countering your champion or your enemy is fed.
Picking „Serylda's grudge“ is recommended whenever you counter your enemy or you are ahead in lane.


Fifth item options are: „Death's Dance“, „Sterak's Gage“ (if it wasn't picked earlier), „Spirit Visage“.


And finally your sixth item options are: „Spirit Visage“ (if it wasn't picked earlier), „Sterak's Gage“ (if it wasn't picked earlier), „Death's Dance“ (if it wasn't picked earlier).

Match-ups

FAQ

Q. What role does Aatrox play in League of Legends?
A. Aatrox is primarily played as a top lane champion. He is a juggernaut with strong healing, sustain, and high damage output. He excels in extended fights and can be a powerful frontline presence.

Q. How does Aatrox's healing work?
A. Aatrox's healing mainly comes from his passive, Deathbringer Stance, and his E (Umbral Dash), which increases healing from all sources. His R (World Ender) also enhances his healing, making him extremely difficult to kill during fights.

Q. What are Aatrox's biggest weaknesses?
A. Aatrox struggles against crowd control (CC) because he relies on his abilities to deal damage and sustain himself. Champions with high mobility can also dodge his Q (The Darkin Blade), reducing his damage potential.

Q. What items should I build on Aatrox?
A. Core items for Aatrox include Goredrinker for sustain, Black Cleaver for armor shredding, and Sterak's Gage for survivability. Situational items include Spirit Visage (for increased healing) and Death's Dance (for better sustain in fights).
