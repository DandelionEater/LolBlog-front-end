Champion's lore

For most of her life, Ahri's origins were a mystery to her, the history of her vastayan tribe all but lost save for the twin gemstones she has carried her entire life. Ahri's earliest memories are of running with icefoxes in the northern reaches of Shon-Xan. Though she knew she was not one of them, they clearly saw her as something of a kindred spirit, and came to accept her within the pack...

General information

Ahri's roles are „Mage“ and „Assassin“.
Difficulty is considered to be „medium“.

Recommended runes

As of today Ahri builds mainly offensive mage items, tanky options depend on your match-up.

Let's start with runes:
Primary rune page is going to be „Sorcery“. Keystone „Summon Aery“ then follow it by choosing „Manaflow band“, „Transcendence“ and finish it off with „Scorch“.
Secondary rune page is going to be „Precision“. Choose „Legend: Haste“, „Cut down“.
As for the masteries, pick „Offense: attack speed“, „Flex: adaptive force“, „Defense: health (flat)“.

Recommended skills

Let's talk skills!
Start by levelling your Q level 1, then W level 2 and E level 3. After that just level your skills in that order „Q > W > E“ and don't forget to put a point on your R skill when reaching levels 6/11/16.

Recommended items

It's time to discuss the itemization!
As we have mentioned before Ahri builds mainly offensive mage items. So let's start with your starting items.
Generally you want to buy a „health potion“ and a „Doran's ring“.


Next is your core items (meaning you have to build them every game).
Start off with „Malignance“ then get „Sorcerer's Shoes“ and „Horizon focus“.


Now we get to items that will be decided based on your match-up.
Your fourth item can be picked in between „Rabadon's deathcap“ or „Mejai's Soulstealer“.
Picking „Rabadon's deathcap“ is recommended whenever your enemy is either countering your champion or your enemy is fed.
Picking „Mejai's Soulstealer“ is recommended whenever you counter your enemy or you are ahead in lane.


Fifth item options are: „Rabadon's deathcap“ (if it wasn't picked earlier), „Mejai's Soulstealer“ (if it wasn't picked earlier), „Zhonya's Hourglass“.


And finally your sixth item options are: „Rabadon's deathcap“ (if it wasn't picked earlier), „Void Staff“, „Mejai's Soulstealer“ (if it wasn't picked earlier).

Match-ups

These 5 champions are going to give you the hardest time during laning phase.
Here they are ranked from 1 to 5 (1 being the hardest laning phase, 5 being least hard):
1. Gragas
2. Fizz
3. Ekko
4. Mel
5. Naafiri
If you ever get these match-ups try your best to farm safely, sometimes poking your enemy just a bit and waiting for your jungler's or team mate's ganks in order to have an all-in battle.


These 5 champions are going to give you the easiest time during laning phase.
Here they are ranked from 1 to 5 (1 being the easiest laning phase, 5 being least easy):
1. Pantheon
2. Irelia
3. Ambessa
4. Smolder
5. Corki
If you ever get these match-ups try your best to zone your enemy from farm, poke the enemy and only go all-in only when your enemy is around 30% health points.
